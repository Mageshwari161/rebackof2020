IPL

The 2020 Indian Premier League Final was played on 10 November 2020 between the Mumbai Indiansand the Delhi Capitals at Dubai International Cricket Stadium, Dubai. It was a Day/Night Twenty20 match, which decided the winner of 2020 season of the Indian Premier League (IPL), an annual Twenty20 tournament in India.The defending champions, the Mumbai Indians, retained the title by winning the match by five wickets. This was the fifth IPL title for the Mumbai Indians, having appeared in the finals six times. This was the first IPL final appearance for the Delhi Capitals.

Indian Super League

The 2020–21 Indian Super League season will be the seventh season of the Indian Super League since its formation and 25th season of the top division of the Indian football league system.
